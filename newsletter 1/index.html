Bhakti Marga's Monthly International News Update, including summaries of last month's events and publications, and updates about upcoming activities.

Jai Gurudev dear friends & family!<

Welcome to the very first edition of the NEW Global Bhakti Marga Monthly Newsletter!

Sri Swami Vishwananda’s mission „Bhakti Marga“ is growing more and more, and the range of events and activities of our family around the world is expanding. Currently, you are used to receive periodic updates via Telegram, blogs, webpages, Youtube, Twitter, Facebook and also email. You may feel that this is more than enough, yet, surprisingly, some important information is not transmitted, and even more importantly, the „bigger picture“ gets somewhat lost inside of all this information flow.

This is why we have chosen to provide you with a monthly update, that completes the circle. It will ensure that you „get“ at least once a month the „musts“ of what has and is about to happen in the world of Sri Swami Vishwananda’s family Bhakti Marga. Enjoy, and please give us feedback. This will help us refine the newsletter for you. Till next month!

Much Love,

Swami Kuru,

on behalf of the BM Media Team, at Shree Peetha Nilaya, Springen
